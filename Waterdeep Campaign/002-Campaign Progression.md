Campaign Progression
====================

This is the order in which to play the scenarios in this campaign. Inbetween each scenario, the "town rules" apply - please refer to Campaign Rules page.

1. Expose Cult Corruption
2. Establish Harpers Safe House
3. Install a Spy in Castle Waterdeep
4. Raid Orc Stronghold
5. Steal from House Adarbrent
6. Defeat Uprising From Undermountain
7. Prison Break
8. Raid on Undermountain
