Scenario 3 - Install a Spy in Castle Waterdeep
==============================================

_“Watch and listen, watch and listen . . .”_


### Components
This scenario uses tiles from **Wrath of Ashardalon** (only regular tiles, not the Horrid Chamber or Dire Chamber ones).
Except for the tiles and special components mentioned below, this scenario uses treasures, monsters and events from **Legend of Drizzt**.

### Special Components in this Adventure
#### From the Wrath of Ashardalon box
* Start tile
* Vault tile
* Tunnel Exit tile
* Margrath figure and villain card
* Three Duergar Guard figures and monster cards

#### From the Legend of Drizzt box
* Catti-Brie figure and ally card

### Game set-up
Take the Vault tile, and shuffle it with two random tiles. 

Take the Tunnel Exit tile, and shuffle it with two random tiles.

Put the tile deck containing the Tunnel Exit tile under the tile deck containing the Vault tile.

Shuffle the remaining tile deck and place these six tiles under the first four tiles of the deck.

Place aside the Margrath figure and villain card.

Place aside the three Duergar Guard figures and monster cards.

Place the Start tile on the table and the two heroes and ally on the tile.

Start the scenario with two healing surges.

For treasures, refer to Campaign Rules page.

### When you start the advendutre
Read the following:
> As soon as you recovered from your last adventure, you have been approached by a secretive woman. "I am looking for a way in to Castle Waterdeep, and not through the front door. I heard you can help me." She asks you to accompany her that very same night  through the network of tunnels leading from the river to the castle. There are many secret paths and entrances to the castle, each with its own dangers..

### When you reveal the Vault tile
Read the following:
> You have stumbled upon a secret vault in the dungeons. An old dwarf with a giant mace looks momentarily surprised when he sees you. It is as if he was expecting someone else. You have a notion that you could talk to him and explain that you mean him no harm. But he immediately attacks you as he growls. "What are you doing here? You will die now!".

### After you have defeated Margrath
Read the following:
> You strike the final blow! But with the last ounce of his strength Margath shouts: "Guards! Guards! Kill these intruders!".

For any new tile they you reveal, place a Duergar Guard instead of drawing a random monster.

### When you reveal the Tunnel Exit tile
Read the following:
> Finally you see the exit leading from these tunnels into the castle. Catti-Brie exclaims "This is it! I can get into the castle from here. Thank you for taking me here!". You say good-bye and she nimbly runs up the steps without looking back.

### Victory conditions
To win this scenario, you need to defeat Margrath and Catti-Brie needs to be on the Tunnel Exit tile.

You lose if a Hero or Ally starts their turn with 0 HP and no surge tokens left.
